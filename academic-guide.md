# Academic & Capstone Project Guide

## Using AAO Data Lab Challenges in Your Classroom

AAO Data Lab challenges are real-world problems with meaningful civic impact — making them ideal for:

- **Capstone projects** (CS, Data Science, Urban Planning, Public Policy)
- **Senior design courses**
- **Hackathons and project fairs**
- **Independent study and thesis work**

## How It Works

1. **Browse** the [Project Gallery](https://github.com/all-aboard-ohio) to find a challenge that fits your course learning objectives.
2. **Contact us** via the AAO Slack (`#education` channel) to register your class or project team.
3. **Fork** the relevant GitHub template repository.
4. **Build** — your team works on the project as a course assignment.
5. **Present** — demo your work to AAO stakeholders (optional but highly encouraged).
6. **Publish** — with your permission, outstanding submissions may be incorporated into production at [lab.allaboardohio.org](https://lab.allaboardohio.org).

## What Students Gain

- Real production experience with civic-impact software
- A portfolio piece backed by a public GitHub repository
- Mentorship from AAO maintainers and volunteer engineers
- Letters of recommendation available for contributors with 3 or more merged PRs

## For Professors

We are happy to co-design a semester-long project rubric tailored to your course goals. Reach out via the [AAO Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/all-aboard-ohio/shared_invite/zt-3wgj180pu-eWAJoGn4_6~y9YHR9Lq3qA) in the `#education` channel.
